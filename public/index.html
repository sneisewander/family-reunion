Sandpearl Resort

500 Mandalay Ave, Clearwater Beach, FL 33767

Our recently renovated 253 guest rooms and suites evoke the relaxed essence of our Gulf Coast setting. Comfort meets style with sunny Floridian-inspired decor and private balconies with sweeping views of Clearwater, Mandalay Bay, and the Gulf of Mexico. Our suites feature full kitchens, dining rooms, and furnished balconies. Sandpearl's laid-back elegance is an ideal home base for your next beach vacation, romantic escape, business trip, or family gathering. — Sandpearl website.

Calypso Queen

Our Tropical Buffet Cruise will be aboard the Calypso Queen!

The Calypso Queen, a 65-foot triple decked ship, specifically designed for cruising in the intracoastal waterways, offers casual sightseeing, luncheon and dinner cruises. This colorfully appointed vessel with a Caribbean theme, boasts 2 dining levels that are enclosed, air-conditioned and contain guest seating. The third level is an open-air deck that serves a variety of functions and provides beautiful views of the passing waterfront. The vessel is complete with restrooms on both dining levels, two full service bars, full service galley and state of the art sound system. — Calypso website.

The Queen has limited capacity, so RSVP to book your spot today!

Who is invited?

All Neisewander family members - direct descendants of Ray Neisewander, Sr., spouses, and adopted family members. Those eligible for a hotel room include family member shareholders and heir-apparent family members, age 18 and older.

What is the cost?

Raynor will cover the cost of your stay at the Sandpearl Resort from June 13 – June 15, 2025 and all Family Reunion events. Raynor will provide a $350 travel stipend and a $100 activity voucher for family shareholders and those eligible for the heir-apparent program. Cost to family members includes travel to and from the Sandpearl Resort, Clearwater Beach and any activities you'd like to do in Clearwater outside of Family Reunion events.

Who is an "heir-apparent" family member?

Lineal descendants of Ray Neisewander, Sr. age 18 and older who are not currently shareholders, but may one day become future shareholders of Raynor stock.

What if I want to extend my stay?

We have a limited number of rooms available for early arrival Thursday, June 12, 2025. Please indicate your desire for early arrival in the registration form. Your $350 travel stipend will be used toward the additional hotel night if you opt to arrive early. Family members will be responsible for accommodations after Sunday, June 15, 2025.

Who is invited to the shareholder orientation and shareholder update meeting?

ALL family members and spouses are invited and encouraged to attend the shareholder orientation and update meetings! Shareholder Orientation is a good refresher for both new and legacy shareholders.

Who should I contact if I have further questions?

Please direct questions to Family Council Chair, Corey Reuter at reuter_67@yahoo.com or 815-440-2149.

What do I do next?!

Register via this website! To RSVP for the Family Reunion, please complete the registration form on this website — this will help us reserve the correct number of rooms and ensure we plan adequately for all events. Registration will close on March 3, 2025, so please be sure to RSVP before then!
