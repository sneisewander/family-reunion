Frequently Asked Questions

Who is invited?

All Neisewander family members - direct descendants of Ray Neisewander, Sr., spouses, and adopted family members. Those eligible for a hotel room include family member shareholders and heir-apparent family members, age 18 and older.

What is the cost?

Cost to family members includes travel to and from the Gaylord Opryland Hotel, Nashville and any activities you'd like to do in Nashville outside of Family Reunion events. Raynor will cover the cost of your stay at the Gaylord Opryland from July 22- July 23 and all Family Reunion events. Raynor will provide a $350 travel stipend and a $100 activity voucher for family shareholders and those eligible for the heir-apparent program.

Who is an "heir-apparent" family member?

Lineal descendants of Ray Neisewander, Sr. age 18 and older who are not currently shareholders, but may one day become future shareholders of Raynor stock.

What if I want to extend my stay in Clearwater?

We have a limited number of rooms available for early arrival Thursday, July 21, 2022. Please indicate your desire for early arrival in the registration form. $250 will be deducted from your travel stipend for each early arrival night. Family members will be responsible for lodging after Sunday, July 24, 2022.

Will there by childcare available?

Unfortunately, the Gaylord Opryland does not offer childcare. Please arrange childcare if needed. Children are welcome to attend the Grand Ole Opry show – we will need to secure a ticket for children age 4 and older; under age four do not require a ticket, but cannot occupy their own seat and must sit on the lap of a parent or guardian.

Who should I contact if I have further questions?

Please direct questions to Family Council Chair, Corey Reuter at reuter_67@yahoo.com or 815-440-2149.

What do I do next?!

Register via this website! To RSVP for the Family Reunion, please complete the registration form on this website — this will help us reserve the correct number of rooms and ensure we plan adequately for all events. Registration will close on April 15, 2022, so please be sure to RSVP before then!
